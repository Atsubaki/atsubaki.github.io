Hello, you reached my Hobbies page. Some of the things I like to do include:

  • Watching Movies and TV: As a kid I always liked to watch moves as a hobby. One of the first ones I watched was Terminator II: Judgement Day The effects were great and the soundtrack got me engaged throughout the entire movie and that gave way to my film addiction today. Currently this has evolved into a Netflix addiction due to my lack of self control when it comes to binging. Some of my favourite series so far include: The Wire The Game of Thrones and The Walking Dead.
  • Playing Games: I play a lot of games since i was a kid and one of my favourite genres is the MMO or "Massive Multiplayer Online". Generally these types of games sport a large player-base and within the game there are mechanics that let these players interact with each other in meaningful ways. By doing so i believe that the individual behind the avatar isn't just one person rather instead he is a person that is pursuing some sort of collective goal with other players. Currently the game I am playing with friends is known as "Black Desert Online".
  • Chatting with Friends: Weather it's in "IRL" or "In Game". I enjoy socializing with others. It is one of my greatest hobbies and often times i do it with the other ones seen above. I believe by connecting with other individuals you are sharing an experience and these experiences will fufill you as a person.
